# Business Requirements

## 1. Identity & Reputation (Trust Pillar)
* **BR-001 (Roblox Identity Integration):** The platform must mandate Roblox OAuth 2.0 integration for all users to ensure accountability. Business entities operating on the platform must be verifiable via their primary Roblox account to mitigate "throwaway" scam accounts.
* **BR-002 (Reputation-Based Access):** Users shall earn "Skill Tiers" and "Champion" status based on verifiable transaction success. This ensures high-value projects are reserved for vetted, high-reputation developers.

## 2. Financial Ecosystem (Economy Pillar)
* **BR-003 (Internal Credit Ledger):** All financial operations must utilize an internal "Credit" currency pegged 1:1 to USDT. This eliminates the technical and legal complexity of direct fiat/crypto transfers at the point of sale.
* **BR-004 (Escrow-as-a-Service):** The platform must act as a neutral third-party custodian, locking funds upon contract initiation and releasing them only upon verified milestone completion or dispute resolution.
* **BR-005 (Transactional Auditability):** The system must maintain an immutable, append-only ledger for every credit movement to support financial transparency and regulatory reporting.

## 3. Marketplace & Collaboration (Operational Pillar)
* **BR-006 (Talent & Project Lifecycle):** The platform must provide a structured workflow for job posting, bidding, and contract management. This moves collaboration away from disorganized Discord channels into a searchable, indexed database.
* **BR-007 (Secure Asset Exchange):** The Marketplace must provide a secure environment for buying/selling digital assets (scripts, models), including automated security scans to detect backdoors before asset transfer.

## 4. Dispute & Community (Risk Pillar)
* **BR-008 (Mediation Framework):** The business must provide a formal dispute resolution channel. This is critical to building professional trust and reducing the platform's long-term "scam-risk" liability.
* **BR-009 (Community Engagement):** A moderated Forum must be available to provide a professional space for community knowledge sharing, restricted to "Active" verified users to ensure high-quality discussion.

## 5. Compliance & Safety
* **BR-010 (Regulatory Alignment):** The platform must enforce strict data minimization practices and provide automated "Right to be Forgotten" workflows to ensure compliance with COPPA/GDPR, given the presence of minors in the Roblox ecosystem.
* **BR-011 (Terms of Service Enforcement):** The platform must maintain robust monitoring systems to identify and remove content that violates Roblox’s platform policies, protecting the business’s standing with Roblox Corporation.
