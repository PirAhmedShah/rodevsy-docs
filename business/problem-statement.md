## 1. Executive Summary

### High-Level Summary of the Problem
Most of the Roblox developers are working in unregulated enviroment such as on Discord servers (like HiddenDevs, Rodevs etc) where majority accounts are scammers. **Client** can scam by not paying the promised amount and **Developer** can scam by not satisfying the customer.

### Target Audience and Proposed Solution
Our target audience are Roblox users who are seeking for safer way to work with remote developer(s)/client(s). The proposed solution is to have a dedicated trusted escrow platform **RoDevsy** for clients and developers so that they can share their work would fear of getting scammed.


## 2. The Core Problem
The core problem is the lack of a trusted platform for Roblox developers and clients to collaborate safely. Currently, most transactions and collaborations occur on unregulated platforms like Discord, where the risk of scams is high. Clients may not receive the promised work after payment, and developers may not get paid after delivering their services. The desired state is to have a secure and reliable escrow platform, RoDevsy, that facilitates safe transactions between clients and developers, ensuring that both parties are protected and satisfied with the outcome.

The current state involves high risks of fraud and mistrust, leading to lost opportunities and dissatisfaction among users. The desired state is a well-regulated environment where users can confidently engage in transactions, knowing that their interests are safeguarded through an escrow system.

## 3. Pain Points
- **User Pain Point 1:** Difficulty in finding trustworthy developers/clients on unregulated platforms.
- **User Pain Point 2:** High risk of scams leading to financial losses and dissatisfaction.
- **User Pain Point 3:** Lack of a formal dispute resolution mechanism in case of disagreements.
- **User Pain Point 4:** Time-consuming processes to verify the credibility of the other party.

## 5. Business Need & Opportunity
Solving this problem is valuable as it addresses a critical gap in the Roblox development ecosystem, enhancing trust and security for both developers and clients. By providing a dedicated escrow platform, RoDevsy can significantly reduce the incidence of scams, leading to increased user satisfaction and retention. This aligns with strategic company goals of fostering a safe and reliable online environment, promoting user engagement, and expanding market reach. The opportunity cost of not solving this problem includes continued user dissatisfaction, loss of potential revenue, and damage to the company's reputation within the Roblox community. We can make potential revenue through transaction fees, premium services, and partnerships with Roblox-related businesses.
