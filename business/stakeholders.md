# Stakeholder Register

This document identifies individuals and organizations impacted by or interested in the development and operation of RoDevsy.

| Stakeholder Name | Role / Title | Engagement | Influence | Interest | Key Expectations | Communication Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pir Ahmed Shah** | Founder & Lead Developer | Internal | High | High | Technical stability, market viability, and successful MVP rollout. | Daily/Weekly syncs; progress reports; architectural reviews. |

| **Early Adopters** | Beta Testers / Power Users | External | Medium | High | Functional tools, intuitive UI/UX, and fast bug resolution. | Dedicated community feedback channels, survey outreach. |
| **Future Strategic Partners** | Roblox Communities/Businesses | External | Medium | Medium | Brand exposure, mutual growth, and credibility within the ecosystem. | Formal partnerships, co-marketing, ecosystem integration. |

---

### Management Strategy
* **High Influence / High Interest**: Stakeholders in this category require active management and close collaboration (e.g., the Founder).
* **High Influence / Medium Interest**: These stakeholders require consistent monitoring to ensure compliance and prevent friction (e.g., Roblox Corporation).
* **Medium Influence / High Interest**: These stakeholders require transparent, frequent communication to maintain engagement and build brand loyalty (e.g., the Roblox Community).

*Note: This register is a living document. As RoDevsy moves from the MVP stage into the Marketplace and Forum rollout phases, this table will be updated to reflect new operational stakeholders.*
