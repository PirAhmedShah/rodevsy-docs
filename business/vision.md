## 1. Vision Statement
Roblox users who seek a secure and trustworthy way to collaborate with developers and clients, RoDevsy is an escrow platform that ensures safe transactions and protects both parties from scams. Unlike unregulated platforms like Discord, our product provides a reliable and regulated environment with robust dispute resolution mechanisms, fostering trust and satisfaction among users. It will also game assets marketplace where users can buy and sell game assets securely. This will differentiate us from other similar platforms by providing a one-stop solution for both escrow services and game asset trading within the Roblox community. 

## 2. Mission
Our mission is to create a secure and reliable escrow platform, RoDevsy, that facilitates safe transactions between Roblox developers and clients. We aim to protect users from scams by implementing a trusted escrow system, ensuring that payments are only released upon satisfactory completion of work. Additionally, we will provide a marketplace for buying and selling game assets, further enhancing the user experience within the Roblox community. Through user-centric design, robust security measures, and effective dispute resolution mechanisms, we strive to build trust and foster long-term relationships among our users. It will also have rating system and reviews for developers and clients to help users make informed decisions when choosing collaborators.

## 3. Strategic Goals
- **Goal 1:** Launch the RoDevsy MVP with core escrow functionalities within 3 months.
- **Goal 2:** Evolve the platform based on user feedback and market needs over the next 6 months. This includes adding advanced features.
- **Goal 3 (SPECULATIVE, WILL BE MODIFED):** Achieve a user base of 10,000 active users and 1,000 successful transactions within the first year of launch.  
- **Goal 4 (SPECULATIVE, WILL BE MODIFED):** Establish partnerships with at least 5 Roblox-related businesses or communities to enhance platform visibility and credibility within 12 months.

## 5. Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
*(Clearly define what this product will *not* do to maintain focus.)*
- The platform will not support non-Roblox related projects or transactions.
- The platform will not provide direct development services; it will only facilitate connections between clients and developers.
- The platform will not handle disputes outside of the escrow transactions facilitated through RoDevsy.
- The platform will not offer free services; all transactions will incur fees to sustain operations.
- The platform will not support fiat currency transactions initially; it will focus on cryptocurrency payments.
- The platform will not provide legal advice or services related to disputes; it will only offer mediation tools and processes.
- The platform will not include social networking features beyond those necessary for project collaboration and communication.
- The platform will not support direct transaction, all transactions will be handled with in-platform credit system.
