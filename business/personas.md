## Persona 1: Roblox Developer
> *"The one who works hard but gets no money."*

- **Role:** Independent Roblox Developer
- **Demographics:** Approximately 16-25 years old, tech-savvy, often a student or early-career professional.
- **Bio & Background:** This persona is passionate about creating games and experiences on Roblox. They often take on freelance projects to earn money but struggle with finding trustworthy clients and ensuring they get paid for their work.
- **Goals & Motivations:**
  - Secure payment for their development work.
  - Build a reputation within the Roblox community.
  - Find reliable clients for ongoing projects. 
  - Expand their portfolio and skills.
  - 
- **Frustrations & Pain Points:**
  - Fear of being scammed by clients who refuse to pay.
  - Difficulty in finding trustworthy clients.
  - Lack of a secure platform for transactions.
  - Uncertainty about project requirements and payment terms.
- **Behaviors & Habits:**
  - Frequently uses Roblox Studio and related development tools.
  - Active in online forums and communities for Roblox developers.
  - Relies on word-of-mouth and social media to find clients.
- **Technical Aptitude:** 
  - Comfortable with technology and online platforms.
  - Familiar with basic financial transactions but wary of complex payment systems.
- **Preferred Communication Channels:**
  - Discord, Roblox forums, email.

## Persona 2: Roblox Client
> *"I need an asset, but I don't know who to trust."* 
- **Role:** Roblox Game Owner/Client
- **Demographics:** Approximately 10-30 years old, often a gamer or entrepreneur looking to enhance their Roblox games.
- **Bio & Background:** This persona owns or manages Roblox games and is looking to improve their games by acquiring assets, scripts, or development services from trusted developers. They may have limited technical knowledge and rely on external help to achieve their game development goals. They could possess Persona 1 but prefer to outsource certain tasks.
- **Goals & Motivations:**
  - Find reliable developers to enhance their games.
  - Ensure secure transactions for purchased services or assets.
  - Improve the quality and appeal of their Roblox games.
  - Build long-term relationships with trustworthy developers.
- **Frustrations & Pain Points:**
  - Difficulty in finding trustworthy developers.
  - Fear of being scammed or receiving subpar work.
  - Lack of a secure platform for transactions.
  - Uncertainty about project timelines and deliverables.
- **Behaviors & Habits:**
  - Regularly plays and manages Roblox games.
  - Active in Roblox communities and forums.
  - Uses social media to connect with developers and other game owners.
- **Technical Aptitude:** 
  - Basic understanding of Roblox and game development.
  - May struggle with complex payment systems.
- **Preferred Communication Channels:**
  - Discord, Roblox forums, email.
