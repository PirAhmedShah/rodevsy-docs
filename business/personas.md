# User Personas

## Persona 1: The Freelance Developer
> *"I create the experiences, I just need to ensure I get paid for my craft."*

* **Role:** Independent Roblox Scripter, Builder, or Modeler.
* **Demographics:** 18–25 years old; students or early-career professionals.
* **Bio & Background:** Passionate about Roblox development. They frequently take on freelance commissions but currently operate in high-risk environments (e.g., Discord servers) where payment security is non-existent. They are looking for professional legitimacy.
* **Goals & Motivations:**
    * Guaranteed compensation upon project delivery.
    * Building a verifiable, high-quality reputation/portfolio.
    * Securing long-term, reliable client relationships.
* **Frustrations & Pain Points:**
    * Frequent encounters with "ghosting" clients or non-payment scams.
    * Wasted time on projects with poorly defined requirements.
    * Lack of a neutral middleman to handle payment disputes.
* **Technical Aptitude:** High; proficient in Roblox Studio, version control, and digital asset management.
* **Preferred Channels:** Discord, RoDevsy platform, professional forums.

## Persona 2: The Project Client
> *"I have a vision for my game, but I need to find the right talent I can actually trust."*

* **Role:** Roblox Game Owner or Entrepreneur.
* **Demographics:** 18–30 years old; game owners or aspiring community managers.
* **Bio & Background:** Manages or owns a Roblox experience and needs to outsource technical work (scripts, assets) to scale their game. They often lack the specific technical skills to verify the quality of work beforehand and are terrified of being scammed by "portfolio-faking" freelancers.
* **Goals & Motivations:**
    * Safely acquiring high-quality assets/scripts without financial risk.
    * Clearly defining and tracking project milestones.
    * Identifying elite talent through verified ratings and reviews.
* **Frustrations & Pain Points:**
    * Difficulty identifying legitimate talent vs. scammers.
    * Receiving subpar work after providing partial or full payment.
    * Lack of clear communication or missed project deadlines.
* **Technical Aptitude:** Moderate; understands the Roblox ecosystem but may lack depth in technical implementation.
* **Preferred Channels:** Discord, RoDevsy platform, Roblox Community Forum.

### Why these personas matter
* **Developer Focus:** RoDevsy protects the Developer by ensuring funds are secured in escrow *before* the work is released.
* **Client Focus:** RoDevsy protects the Client by holding funds until the agreed-upon milestones are met and verified.
